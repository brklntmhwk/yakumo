Niwatazumi is the steadfast home server and central NAS of the Yakumo architecture. It is the deep, enduring pool that catches and preserves the ever-flowing streams of my digital life. Running silently within the current, it provides the persistent storage and foundational gravity that keeps the ephemeral parts of the network grounded.
